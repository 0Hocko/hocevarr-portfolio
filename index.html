About

I started as the only sysadmin keeping a 24/7 gaming platform healthy. No team, no safety net, just me and about 15 servers serving 90 plus users around the clock. That taught me to build things that don't need me standing over them: solid monitoring, tested backups, and documentation that outlives me.

These days I maintain infrastructure for up to 20 business clients, each running a different setup. Hyper-V here, Proxmox there, ESXi somewhere else. I adapt fast, and I write things down, so the next person, often future me, isn't left guessing. Along the way I also led the build-out of a test environment and the design of a production Kubernetes platform.
